Free Legacy X-Wing League

The Free Legacy X-Wing League is a weekly league that plays the X-Wing Miniatures Game with Fantasy Flight Game's last rules (2.0). We use points from https://xwing-legacy.com/ which have been updated to have points for new releases and further balanced by the X-Wing 2.0 community.
